Metro Line 1 (Ben Thanh - Suoi Tien)

The HCMC Metro system is currently operating on Line 1, connecting central District 1 to Suoi Tien in Di An City. More lines will be developed in future phases.

  • L1-01 — Ben Thanh
  • L1-02 — Ho Chi Minh Opera House
  • L1-03 — Ba Son

  • L1-05 — Tan Cang
  • L1-06 — Thao Dien

  • L1-08 — Rach Chiec
  • L1-09 — Phuoc Long
  • L1-10 — Binh Thai
  • L1-11 — Thu Duc
  • L1-12 — Saigon Hi-tech Park
  • L1-13 — National University
  • L1-14 — Suoi Tien Terminal
